HIT Instructions
You will be shown a search query and a web page. You will then be asked to answer some questions regarding the page and its content. Assume that you were to issue the given query to a search engine (for example Google) and were shown the corresponding webpage as search result, we ask you 9 questions regarding the some things. They mainly revolve around the following:
  • Relevance: The given webpage is relevant if it provides information requested in the query. For example, if the query is 'president of USA' and the page is about 'Barak Obama', it is considered relevant, on the other hand if the page is about 'Cotton candy', it is irrelevant.
  • Given the webpage, we would like you to indicate how easy or difficult was it for you to read the webpage. Was the webpage text clear and easy to read. Whether the length or language of the webpage impacted its readability.
  • We also ask you to mention how easy was it to find the information required to answer the query from the webpage. If you spent alot of time trying to find the information, please also provide the reason for your response.
  • We ask you to rate your understanding of content in web page. Was the webpage text too vague or had too many images/ads/tables that made it difficult to understand it. Where ever possible please provide factors that affected your understanding.
The above parameters are also explained in detail under 'Instructions' on each page. Please click on 'Instructions' button to view them, in case you forget.
