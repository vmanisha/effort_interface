HIT Instructions
You will be shown a search query and a web page. Assume that you were to issue the given query to a search engine (for example Google) and were shown the corresponding webpage as search result, find the answer of query in document where it is available. You should mark all portions of the page that are relevant to the query. Once you have found the answer in webpage, answer 4 questions regarding the page. These aspects are explained in detail below:
  • Findability: We ask you to mention whether or not you were able to find the required information in page.
  • Satisfaction: We ask you to mention how satisfied or happy you were with the page given the query.
  • Highlights: You should mark all the relevant parts of webpage. Highlighting is only necessary when page is relevant to the query. The image shows how to highlight parts of page. You shall only be payed if you mark text that contains the answer to the query.

There are some buttons on top of document for your convenience. They are explained below:
  • Instructions: If you forget instructions, please click on 'Instructions' button to view them.
  • View: To view the text you highlighted, you can click 'View' button.
  • Remove: To remove the text you highlighted, you can click 'Remove' button. Note that it removes all highlights.

Enable javascript on your browser to complete this HIT. Do not refresh the page or press back. Contact the Requester if something is not clear.
NOTE: We track whether or not you read the document. If our system detects that you are cheating, you shall be disqualified and not payed for the hits.

HIT Payment
This HIT consists of 10 query-webpage pairs. Please note that you will be payed ONLY when you COMPLETE QUESTIONS FOR ALL query-webpage pairs. You may choose to skip a query and webpage if you cannot answer any question. If too many query-webpage pairs are skipped, you may not be payed in full.
